The Shopboost plugin will implement the Shopboost tag into your website, so you can make use of the Shopboost service. Go to 'Shopboost' menu option, fill in your merchant id and click the save button.

== Description ==

<strong>Shopboost has a free and paid package, create an account at www.shopboostapp.com.</strong>

- Many webshop visitors only take a look, hesitate and do not buy. Surprise these hesitating visitors and sell more.
- Choose a surprise yourself. Think of a free product, extra service or advice, free shipping or a discount code.
- Due to the advanced hesitating detection, only hesitating visitors are offered the surprise.

> "On average, 30% of the hesitaters still place the order after receiving the surprise."

<strong>Why Shopboost?</strong>
Every webshop suffers from hesitating visitors. Visitors drop out during the ordering process, leave a full shopping cart, keep comparing between different webshops, etc. Shopboost detects this behavior and automatically offers the hesitating visitor a surprise. Think of a free product, extra service or advice, free shipping or a discount code. In this way you enthuse visitors automatically and sell more.

== Installation ==

Just install from your WordPress "Plugins > Add New" screen and all will be well. Manual installation is very straightforward as well:

1. Upload the zip file and unzip it in the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Go to `Shopboost` menuoption at the left side
1. Fill in your `Shopboost` merchant id and choose which options you want to enable.
